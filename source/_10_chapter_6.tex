输入$e(t)$和输出$u(t)$的关系:
\begin{equation}
    u(t)=K_{p} e(t)+K_{i} \int_{0}^{t} e(\tau) d \tau+K_{d} \frac{d e(t)}{d t}
\end{equation}

所以最终可以得到式\eqref{eqn:51}
\begin{equation}
    \label{eqn:51}
    u(k)=K_{p} e_{k}+K_{i} \sum_{i=1}^{k} e(i) \Delta t+K_{d} \frac{e(k)-e(k-1)}{\Delta t}
\end{equation}
